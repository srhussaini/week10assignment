About Us

Located off Highway 108 within a forested valley of the High Sierra Mountains, this charming farmhouse-chic B&B is 2 miles from Twain Harte Lake, and 7 miles from Black Oak Casino.
The warmly decorated country-style rooms are individually styled with wood furnishings and wood-burning stoves; some offer 4-poster beds. All have forest views, flat-screens and free Wi-Fi, as well as iPod docks. Some rooms have private balconies.
Amenities include full breakfast served in a relaxed dining room with antiques, plus a daily wine and cheese reception near the fireplace. In-room massages are available (fee.)
